# Integrations Overview

Nomad AI acts as the central hub, intelligently aggregating data from a diverse range of commerce platforms and seamlessly syncing it with leading financial tools and secure virtual data rooms (VDRs). Our goal is to provide a single, unified view of your business's economic health and prepare you for every stage of your growth journey.

This section provides a detailed look at all our current and planned integrations, outlining their functionality and current development status.
