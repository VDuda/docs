The Amazon Seller integration aims to provide a comprehensive view of your Amazon marketplace activities within Nomad AI.

## Planned Features

*   **FBA Inventory Tracking:** Monitor your Fulfilled by Amazon (FBA) inventory levels and movements.
*   **Multi-Region Sales Aggregation:** Consolidate sales data from various Amazon marketplaces (e.g., US, EU) into a single report.
*   **Profitability Analysis:** Break down fees and costs to understand true Amazon profitability.

## Current Status

*   The integration card is visible in the Nomad AI UI.
*   Backend implementation is in the planning phase.
